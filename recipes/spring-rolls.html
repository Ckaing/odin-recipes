This is a recipe for Vietnamese Fresh Spring Rolls

These fresh Vietnamese spring rolls are a refreshing change from the usual fried variety and have become a family favorite. They are a lovely, light appetizer and delicious dipped in one or both of the sauces included in this recipe. Great for parties as you can prep them ahead of time.

Ingredients

(for 1 serving)

  • Rice noodles
  • Rice wrappers
  • Large cooked shrimp
  • Leaves lettuce
  • Chopped fresh mint
  • Chopped fresh cilantro
  • Chopped fresh thai basil

Steps

  1. Cook the noodles
  2. Soften each wrapper with warm water then fill with ingredients
  3. Fold and roll each spring roll
  4. Make the sauces to serve with the spring rolls
